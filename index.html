Important Info: Local Rules

Alcohol & Local Conduct

Please be aware of the local rules:

  • Drinking alcohol in public (streets, parks, etc.) is forbidden.
  • It is perfectly fine to buy alcohol (e.g., at a Carrefour supermarket) and drink it at our private Riad.
  • Licensed restaurants and bars also serve alcohol.
  • Being drunk and "wasted" in public is not allowed and can lead to trouble. Please be respectful.
